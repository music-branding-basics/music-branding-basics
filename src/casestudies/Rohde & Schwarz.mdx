Rohde & Schwarz is a leading global manufacturer of electronic measurement instruments for electrical, electronic and communications engineering. The company's product portfolio includes oscilloscopes, vector network analyzers, spectrum analyzers, power meters, cable and antenna analyzers, and wireless communication testers. Rohde & Schwarz also offers a wide range of solutions for monitoring, analyzing and securing digital communications.
