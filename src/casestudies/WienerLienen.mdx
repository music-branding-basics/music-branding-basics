Wiener Linien is the public transit authority in Vienna, Austria. It operates over 140 bus, tram and metro lines in the city, with over 1.3 million passenger trips daily. Wiener Linien is one of the most efficient and punctual public transit systems in Europe, with a 97% on-time record.
