The FIAT 500e is an electric car produced by FIAT. The 500e is based on the FIAT 500 and was first introduced in 2013. The 500e has a range of up to 80 miles and a top speed of 85 mph. The 500e is available in two trims: the Base and the Lounge. The Base trim includes features such as 16-inch alloy wheels, automatic climate control, and a six-speaker audio system. The Lounge trim adds features such as 17-inch alloy wheels, a sunroof, and a premium audio system.
