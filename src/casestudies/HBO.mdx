HBO is an American premium cable and satellite television network that is owned by WarnerMedia Studios & Networks and is the flagship property of WarnerMedia. The company has produced some of the most groundbreaking and popular shows in television history, such as The Sopranos, Sex and the City, and Game of Thrones. HBO is also known for its award-winning films, documentaries, and sports programming.
